How to use this website

Enter a word in the search bar to see its performance in the North American stock markets over the past 20 years!


How this website works!

This website uses a database of stock metadata (descriptions) I have collected and searches through 9000+ stocks on the NYSE, NASDAQ and TSX to collect those that relate to the word you search. It then acts as though you have chosen all those stocks to be equally weighted in a portfolio, since the first occurrence of the word to the last. This creates the words "performance" over time, and can yield some extremely interesting results based on what you search!
